Our Story

Founded in 2022, Besisahar BBQ Park Restaurant & Lodge has been serving authentic barbecue and providing comfortable accommodations in the heart of the mountains. Our passion for slow-smoked meats and warm hospitality has made us a beloved destination for locals and travelers alike.

We use only the finest cuts of meat, seasoned with our secret blend of spices and smoked low and slow over hickory wood. Our lodge offers a peaceful retreat where you can relax and enjoy the natural beauty of the surrounding mountains.
